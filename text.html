CRM For Better Team Work Collaboration

Internal Team Chat & Mail

CRMs include an internal chat enabling team members to communicate in real time without needing to switch to another app. This promotes smoother and faster collaboration.

Scheduled Work Plan

Scheduled Work Plan

Sales managers can assign specific tasks for each team member. It enables managers to clearly communicate expectations and milestones, helping team members understand their priorities and objectives.

Attendance Tracking

The attendance feature within a CRM allows salespeople to log their presence, creating a digital record of attendance in real-time. This feature can include GPS-enabled check-ins, especially useful for field sales reps.
