Perfect Task Management Solution for Every Team

Pro-Task is made to fit the needs of any team, big or small, in any industry.This task tracking tool helps your team stay organized, meet deadlines, and collaborate smoothly. It’s simple, easy to use, and designed to make tasks.

Organized Workflow with Hierarchy-Based Task Management
Pro-Task helps you manage tasks with a clear hierarchy structure, ensuring an organized workflow across your team or organization.
Director
Manage and reopen tasks across branches and departments, with the ability to assign, close, and reopen tasks for any user.
HOD, Supervisor & Senior Manager
Manage tasks across departments from the allocated branch by creating personal or team tasks, assigning, closing, reopening subordinate tasks, and closing multiple tasks simultaneously.
Associate
Associates can create and work on assigned tasks, update progress visible to the reporting manager, and mark tasks as resolved.

Manage Tasks with Ease Using Pro-Task

Pro-Task is your go-to task management software, designed to make task organization, scheduling, and tracking simple and effective. Packed with powerful features, it ensures your team stays productive and aligned with their goals

Task Creation

  • Users can effortlessly create tasks by adding a title, open and close dates, detailed descriptions, and remarks for clarity. This ensures every task is well-documented and easy to follow.
  • Assign tasks with priority levels—Regular, Important, or Critical—so your team knows which tasks require immediate attention. Focus on what matters most to stay productive.
  • Set clear timelines for each task, ensuring smoother execution and helping your team meet deadlines with ease

Task Assignment

  • Assign tasks by selecting the appropriate branch, role, and user to ensure the right person is responsible for each task.
  • Add relevant documents or files while assigning tasks to provide additional context and instructions, leaving no room for confusion.
  • All users, except associates, can assign or reassign tasks as needed to adapt to shifting priorities or workloads, ensuring that work progresses smoothly without interruptions.

Internal Communication with Team

  • Use in-task chat and file sharing to communicate seamlessly with your team. This ensures all discussions stay connected to their respective tasks for clarity and context.
  • Eliminate the need for external tools by using Pro-Task’s integrated messaging and sharing options. Keep everything in one place to save time and improve efficiency.
  • Maintain all task-related conversations in a centralized location for easy reference. This ensures you can quickly revisit past discussions when needed.

Dynamic Task Status and Task Completion Indicator

  • Keep track of task progress with dynamic status updates, such as "In Progress," "Pending," or "Completed." This ensures everyone is aligned on the current state of each task.
  • Use intuitive progress indicators to see how close a task is to completion.
  • Easily identify tasks that are overdue, on schedule, or completed ahead of time. This clarity enables better performance tracking and workflow optimization.
